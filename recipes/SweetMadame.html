Sweet Madame

Honey-roasted fowl. The honest and sweet flowers come together to compliment the tender fowl meat.

Credit to: Genshin Impact

Ingredients Needed:

  • 2 fowl
  • 2 sweet flower

Instructions:

  1. Coat the chicken in sweet flower honey
  2. Roast the chicken in an oven
  3. Garnish the chicken with lime or other preferred garnish
