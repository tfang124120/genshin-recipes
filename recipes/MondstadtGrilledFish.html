Mondstadt Grilled Fish

Fish grilled thoroughly with moderate fire, making it completely dry and cripsy. The sprinkled pepper on top brings out the freshness of the fish.

Credit to: Genshin Impact

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 fish
  • 1 pepper
  • 1 cabbage

Instructions:

  1. Dice the fish into large one inch chunks
  2. Chop up the cabbage into large chunks
  3. Skewer the diced fish and roast over an open fire
  4. Rotate the skewer while sprinkling pepper over the fish
  5. Place the cooked fish and cabbage on a skewer in an alternating pattern
